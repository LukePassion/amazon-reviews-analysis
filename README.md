Amazon Reviews Analysis

Project Overview

This project analyzes customer reviews from Amazon to uncover patterns in customer satisfaction, 
sentiment trends, and behavioral insights over time.

The goal is to transform raw textual data into meaningful business insights that can support decision-making 
and improve customer experience.

⸻

Objectives
	•	Analyze the distribution of customer ratings
	•	Identify sentiment patterns in reviews
	•	Understand customer behavior through review length
	•	Detect trends in customer satisfaction over time
	•	Extract key insights from textual data

⸻

Technologies Used
	•	Python
	•	PySpark
  •	SQL (Databricks)
	•	Pandas
	•	Matplotlib
⸻

Key Insights
	•	A strong predominance of negative reviews was identified, with rating 1 being the most frequent.
	•	Negative sentiment dominates the dataset, indicating widespread customer dissatisfaction.
	•	Negative reviews tend to be significantly longer, suggesting higher emotional engagement from dissatisfied customers.
	•	A major shift in sentiment occurs after 2018, where negative reviews become more frequent.
	•	The average rating shows a consistent decline over time, dropping from ~4.5 (2007–2015) to ~1.5 (2020–2024).

⸻

Business Impact

The analysis suggests a clear deterioration in customer experience over time.

This trend may be associated with operational scaling challenges, customer service issues, or logistical inefficiencies.

⸻

Recommendations
	•	Investigate the root causes of increased negative feedback after 2018
	•	Improve customer service processes
	•	Monitor customer feedback continuously
	•	Implement alert systems for spikes in dissatisfaction
	•	Analyze recurring negative keywords

⸻

Future Improvements
	•	Build an interactive dashboard (Power BI)
	•	Apply Machine Learning for sentiment classification
	•	Perform segmentation analysis (products, regions, users)
	•	Develop real-time monitoring systems

⸻

Conclusion

This project demonstrates how data analysis can uncover valuable insights about customer behavior 
and business performance.

By combining data cleaning, exploratory analysis, and visualization, it was possible to identify
a significant decline in customer satisfaction over time.
